About me

Distributed systems done right

I build backends that don't fall over when 10,000 users show up at once. With a Research Master's in Distributed Computing from University of Sousse and 7+ years in production environments, I've developed a taste for solving problems that most engineers avoid — high concurrency, event-driven architecture, real-time systems at scale.

I've shipped platforms used daily in Saudi Arabia, Oman, UAE and Tunisia — across industries like logistics, ERP, fintech, and e-commerce. I don't just write code; I own the full delivery cycle from architecture decisions to Nginx configs to Grafana dashboards.

Trilingual in Arabic, French and English. I thrive equally in Gulf and European project contexts — which means I understand the market, the expectations, and the compliance requirements (yes, ZATCA included 😄).
